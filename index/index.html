AreoLeds

AreoLeds is a company based in Boise, Idaho that manufactures LED bulbs for aircrafts. In September of 2018 they asked me to add functionality to their website for product warranties. This means adding a front-end page so that users can input and send their product information, and then store that warranty information on a backend. I finished in the following November, and now it is easier than for the employees at AreoLeds to manage their warranties as well as customers to send their own.
